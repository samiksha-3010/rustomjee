The Project

A Towering Silhouette
of Sophistication

Empire Preston is designed for those who seek a life that blends ease, elegance, and everyday connection. Rising 10 storeys high with two thoughtfully planned wings, it offers a seamless mix of 1, 2 & 2.5 BHK residences, select commercial spaces on the ground level, and tower parking for convenience. Set in a well-connected neighbourhood, it keeps you close to essentials, business hubs, and lifestyle destinations, while still giving you the comfort of a private, secure community. Empire Preston is more than a home - it's a place where families grow, neighbours connect, and life feels complete.
